This research initiative aims to establish a research community of Mental Well-being Intelligence (MWI), which strives to achieve a deep understanding of human mental health through the combined analysis of quantitative and qualitative data. MWI also aims to accelerate the development and deployment of technology to enhance mental well-being. We also aim at developing two types of next-generation researchers: Mental Health Computer Scientists, who are equipped with clinical psychological knowledge and conduct computer science research on mental health; and Information Psychologists, who hold prototyping capabilities for information systems and quantitative data analysis methods and bring them to the field of clinical psychology. These researchers will become the key players to directly address the global challenge of human mental health.
